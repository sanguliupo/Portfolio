About me

I'm a Web Developer with double master degrees in Civil Engineering and Creative Writing. I build full-stack web apps in JavaScript, React, HTML, CSS, Node.js, Express, SQL, and NoSQL.

Born in Puerto Rico to Taiwanese parents, I spent my youth in both PR and Taiwan. I'm a published short story writer, and can speak English, Chinese, Spanish, and Japanese.
